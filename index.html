SURVIVAL TOP DOWN SHOOTER

Game Description

In Survival Top-Down Shooter, you must fight for your life against relentless waves of enemies. Armed with your weapon, you’ll need quick reflexes and strategic movement to dodge enemy attacks while eliminating them before they overwhelm you. As you score points, the enemies become faster and more aggressive, pushing your survival skills to the limit. How long can you last in this intense battle for survival?

Game Controls

  • Move Forward: W
  • Move Back: S
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • R: Restart
